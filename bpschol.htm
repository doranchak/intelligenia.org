BP - Visiting Research Scholars

In December 2000 The Bletchley Park Trust announced the appointment of its three charter Visiting Research Scholars.


From L to R: Ralph Erskine of Belfast, Northern Ireland - the prominent Naval historian; Frode Weierud of Geneva, Switzerland - a leading authority on German and other cipher machines; David Hamer of Princeton, NJ, USA - cryptologist and cipher machine researcher.
