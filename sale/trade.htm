Cipher Machines etc. for Sale or Trade

From time to time I acquire a cipher machine or other crypto-related artefact  for sale or trade. I will also act as agent for any collector who wishes to dispose of items - they will be listed on this page as they appear. I receive many enquiries in the course of a year from individuals and institutions seeking such collectibles. If you have anything of cryptologic interest - machines, manuals, etc. - that you would like either to sell or to have me sell or exchange on your behalf, please get in touch with me.
